This section contains solutions to selected problems from Artin's Algebra, 2nd ed. I plan on skipping most of the chapters; I'm only here for the finite groups, rings, and fields. I have also skipped certain sections that I didn't find interesting or useful, as well as certain problems (especially those that seemed too tedious). But I suspect that every single problem in Artin has a solution on the web somewhere, so don't despair...

I use the convention that 0 is a natural number, Artin be damned.

Feel free to email me with errata, or to contribute better solutions that can be understood using material covered by the appropriate point in the text.
